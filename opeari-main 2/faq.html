Safety is our highest priority. We take a multi-layered approach:

üõ°Ô∏è Our 4-Layer Safety System
  • Community Verification: Invite-only, locally verified members
  • Background Checks: Comprehensive screening for all care providers
  • Human Moderation: Real people review all profiles and interactions

Additional Safety Features:

  • Reference validation and community feedback
  • Secure messaging with moderation
  • Emergency contact systems

Remember: You're not connecting with strangers ‚Äî you're building relationships with people you choose to trust.

All care providers go through our comprehensive verification process:

  • Criminal background checks at national and local levels
  • Sex offender registry screening
  • Reference validation with previous families
  • Identity verification with government-issued ID
  • Driving record checks for transportation providers

Families can also request additional screening based on their comfort level. We believe in transparency ‚Äî you'll know exactly what checks have been completed for each caregiver.

‚è±Ô∏è Processing Timeline

Initial verification takes 3-5 business days. We also conduct annual re-verification to ensure ongoing safety standards.

We have comprehensive emergency procedures in place:

üö® Emergency Contact Information
  • Child Emergency: Call 911 first, then notify parents
  • Safety Concerns: Opeari Safety Hotline: 1-800-OPEARI-1
  • Platform Issues: support@opeari.com or in-app chat
  • After-hours Support: 24/7 emergency line available

Our Emergency Response Team:

  • Immediate incident response within minutes
  • Coordination with local authorities when needed
  • Family notification and support
  • Follow-up and incident investigation

Our review system is designed for authenticity and trust:

  • Verified Reviews Only: Only families who have actually used a caregiver can leave reviews

  • Fraud Detection: Advanced systems detect fake or manipulated reviews

We also encourage families to conduct their own interviews and reference checks. Reviews are just one tool in your decision-making process.

Legal & Terms

As a family using childcare services, you have several important legal considerations:

üìã Key Legal Responsibilities
  • Employment Classification: Understand if caregivers are employees or contractors
  • Tax Obligations: Potential household employee taxes and reporting
  • Insurance Coverage: Verify coverage for caregivers in your home
  • Workers' Compensation: May be required depending on your state and arrangement

We Recommend:

  • Consulting with a tax professional for guidance
  • Reviewing your homeowner's/renter's insurance policy
  • Understanding your state's household employee laws
  • Using written agreements for all care arrangements

Opeari's Role: We provide educational resources and contract templates, but we are not a legal or tax advisor. Professional consultation is always recommended.

We have a comprehensive dispute resolution process designed to resolve issues fairly and quickly:

ü§ù Resolution Process
  1. Direct Communication: We encourage families and caregivers to communicate first
  2. Mediation Support: Our team provides neutral mediation assistance
  3. Community Review: Community leaders may help with perspective
  4. Formal Resolution: Structured process for serious disputes

Types of Support We Provide:

  • Resolution documentation

Escalation for Serious Issues:

  • Safety concerns are handled immediately
